# Industries Cadre AI Works With

Cadre works with B2B organizations across a range of industries, including:

- Professional services
- Private equity and PE-backed companies
- Financial services
- Mortgage and lending
- Real estate
- Construction
- Manufacturing and logistics
- Retail and e-commerce
- Hospitality

The best fit depends less on the industry label and more on the workflows, decisions, and operational problems you want to improve. Two companies in the same industry can have very different opportunities depending on how they operate.

Cadre also organizes solutions by department — including sales, marketing, customer success, executive leadership, finance, operations, technology, and legal — so AI can be applied where it matters most across the business.

If you'd like to explore whether Cadre is a fit for your situation, a brief guided discovery can suggest a likely starting point, or you can request a conversation with an AI strategist.
