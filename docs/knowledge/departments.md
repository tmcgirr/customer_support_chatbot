# AI Solutions by Department

Cadre applies AI across the business, department by department, so improvements land where teams actually work. Common departments include:

- **Sales** — automate prospecting, qualify leads, and speed up follow-up.
- **Marketing** — generate and test campaigns faster and measure ROI.
- **Customer Success** — predict at-risk accounts and support expansion.
- **Executive Leadership** — synthesize information for faster, better decisions.
- **Finance** — automate reconciliation and close the books faster.
- **Operations** — automate workflows to run leaner and move faster.
- **Technology** — automate code review and repetitive engineering work.
- **Legal** — accelerate contract review and legal research.

The right starting point depends on the workflows, decisions, and operational problems you most want to improve. A brief guided discovery can suggest a likely starting point, or you can request a conversation with an AI strategist.
