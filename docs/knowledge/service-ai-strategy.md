# AI Strategy

AI Strategy helps organizations discover how AI can improve their business: identify and prioritize valuable AI opportunities, understand readiness and constraints, and build a practical roadmap.

The focus is on separating high-ROI opportunities from distractions: understanding which workflows, decisions, and operational problems are worth improving, what the organization is ready to take on, and how to sequence the work so it delivers real value. Cadre often runs this as a structured AI Transformation Intensive that takes a team from zero clarity to a prioritized roadmap they can execute.

AI Strategy is often the most relevant starting point when the goal is prioritization, a roadmap, or assessing readiness before committing to a build.

A strategist would need to understand your systems, data, and constraints before defining a specific engagement. You can request a conversation with an AI strategist to go further.
