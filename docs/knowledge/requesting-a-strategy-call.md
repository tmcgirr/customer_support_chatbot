# Requesting a Strategy Call

If you'd like to talk with a person, Cadre can help you request a conversation with an AI strategist. This is the natural next step for exploring services, scoping an engagement, discussing the AI Maturity Index, or reviewing security and model-selection questions specific to your organization.

Requesting a call opens a short form that sends your request to the right team. It collects a small amount of information — your name, work email, and company, plus the reason for your request, with industry and region optional.

Before anything is sent, you can review your details and confirm. After you submit, you receive a reference for the request.

You can ask to speak with a person at any point in the conversation.
