# How Cadre Scopes Engagements

Cadre engagements are scoped to the business problem. What an engagement involves depends on factors such as the number of workflows, the systems involved, data requirements, implementation complexity, and the level of organizational support required.

Because these factors vary from one organization to the next, engagements are shaped around your specific situation rather than a fixed package. A strategist would need to understand your systems, data, and constraints before defining an engagement.

The best way to explore what an engagement could look like for you is to request a conversation with an AI strategist.
