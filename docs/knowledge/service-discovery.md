# Finding the Right Cadre Service

Cadre can help identify a likely starting point among its services. This is a brief guided process, and the result is a preliminary recommendation rather than a formal project scope.

A few short questions help point to the right service:

1. What business process, decision, or problem are you trying to improve?
2. Which team is most affected?
3. Are you trying to identify opportunities, align leadership, build a solution, or build an AI agent?

How goals typically map to services:

- **Prioritization, roadmap, or readiness** → AI Strategy
- **Alignment, governance, or adoption** → AI Leadership & Facilitation
- **A defined build or integration** → AI Engineering
- **Repeatable task execution using tools** → AI Agents

Any recommendation from this process is preliminary. A strategist would need to understand your systems, data, and constraints before defining an engagement, so the natural next step is to request a strategy call.
